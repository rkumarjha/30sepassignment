Kinner Kailash is a mountain in the Kinnaur district of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is one of the five mount Kailash and is believed to be the abode of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati1. It is located near the Indo-Tibetan border at a height of 17200 ft above sea level2. It is also known for its 79 ft tall shivalinga, which changes colours every second2.
